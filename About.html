About Us

Welcome to NTR1M SKATES! We are passionate about providing professional and flexible skating experiences tailored to skaters of all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced skater, we’re here to help you excel in your skating journey.

Why Choose Us?

At NTR1M SKATES, we focus on delivering top-notch skating lessons with a personalized approach. Our team of experienced coaches ensures that each session is tailored to meet your needs and help you grow as a skater. Whether you aim to skate competitively or recreationally, we have you covered!

Where are we located?

We proudly operate at several key locations, including the iconic Kallang Stadium, the serene Pasir Ris Park, and many other accessible venues across the region. Recognizing the diverse needs of our clients, we also offer highly personalized skating lessons tailored to your preferences. Our dedicated team is committed to making skating lessons as convenient as possible by bringing our expertise directly to your doorstep. Whether you prefer the comfort of your neighborhood or a specific location that fits your schedule, we ensure that every session is designed to suit your unique requirements, delivering both flexibility and exceptional learning experiences.
