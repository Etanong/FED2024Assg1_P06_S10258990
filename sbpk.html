SBPK Slide

Established in 2023, SBPK Slide is a passionate team of youth inline freestyle sliders from Singapore. Committed to excellence, the team embodies the spirit of determination, discipline, and unity. With a relentless drive to succeed, SBPK Slide is willing to train tirelessly and give their all to achieve victory in competitions. The team thrives on pushing boundaries, honing their skills, and representing Singapore on both local and international stages. SBPK Slide isn’t just a sports team; it’s a testament to the power of dedication and the love for the sport.
