About Us

Welcome to NTR1M SKATES

At NTR1M SKATES, our goal is to offer a professional and flexible skating experience that caters to skaters of all levels. Founded in 2024, we are committed to making skating accessible, convenient, and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of location or schedule. Our mission is to deliver high-quality skating lessons tailored to your needs—anywhere, anytime. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned skater, we strive to empower you with the skills and confidence to excel in your skating journey.
