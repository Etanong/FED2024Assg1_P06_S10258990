PACKAGES

Basic

Our basic class are tailored for complete beginner eager to explore skating while building confidence step by step. Throughout the sessions, participants will develop key skills like maintaining safety, gliding smoothly, stopping effectively, and executing basic turns. Embrace the joy of skating as you master the fundamentals!

Skills covered:

  1. Basic walking
  2. rollling
  3. Turning
  4. Braking

Price : $100

Lessons : 4 x 1hr each

Intermediate

Our intermediate class is ideal for skaters who have completed the basic level and are ready to advance their skills. In this course, students will focus on enhancing their balance, turning, braking, and speed. They will also gain the confidence to navigate outdoor spaces independently, learning essential safety practices and techniques for urban skating. Step up your skating game and confidently explore new environments!

Skills covered:

  1. Advance skating
  2. Advance turning
  3. Advance breaking
  4. 1 foot balance
  5. Urban skating
  6. Navigation through obstacles

Price : $380

Lessons : 12 x 1hr each

Advance

Our advanced class is designed for skaters seeking to push their limits and take their skills to the next level. In this course, students will master advanced techniques like speed skating and two-wheel balancing. They will also have the opportunity to explore various skating disciplines, including inline floorball, speed skating, and more. Challenge yourself and discover the versatility of inline skating!

Skills covered:

  1. Backwards skating
  2. Going up and down ramps
  3. 2 wheel balance
  4. Speed skating
  5. Inline floorball
  6. Basic Slalom
  7. basic slide

Price : $440

Lessons : 12 x 1hr each
